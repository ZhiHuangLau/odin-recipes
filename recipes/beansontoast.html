Beans On Toast

Ingredients

  • 2 pieces of bread, any kind
  • 1 400g can Heinz Baked Beans
  • Salt, to taste
  • Black pepper, to taste
  • Paprika flakes, to taste

Steps

  1. First, toast the bread using a toaster or a pan.
  2. Heat up the baked beans in a saucepan and add the seasonings.
  3. Place the toast on a plate and pour the baked beans over it.

If you're feeling fancy, why not add some scrambled eggs?
