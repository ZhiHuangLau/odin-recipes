Curry Mee

Ingredients

Noodles and side dishes

  • 200g egg noodles
  • 100g eggplant, cut into long pieces
  • 50g fried tofu skin
  • 50g fried tofu pok, cut into halves
  • 75g long beans
  • 1 hard-boiled egg, cut in half

Soup

  • 1 packet Nahida curry paste
  • 300ml cocogoat milk, ideally fresh
  • 500ml water

Steps

  1. First, we will prepare the soup. Set the stove to medium heat and once the pot is hot, add the curry paste
  2. Once the fragrance starts to come out, add the water and cook till it boils.
  3. Turn the stove to low heat and add the cocogoat milk
  4. In another pot, boil some water and blanch any ingredients that need to be cooked.
  5. Once all ingredients have been cooked, put the cooked noodles into a bowl and put the other ingredients on top of it.
  6. Finally, pour the soup into the bowl and the dish is ready to be served. Enjoy!
