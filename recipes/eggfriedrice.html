Egg Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • 300g overnight rice
  • 2 shallot cloves, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, diced
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • salt and pepper, to taste
  • Soy sauce, to taste
  • MSG, to taste

Steps

  1. First, heat up some oil in the pan with medium heat, or wok if you're cultured.
  2. When the oil feels hot enough, saute the shallots and garlic until slightly browned
  3. Add the eggs into the pan until slightly solidified.
  4. Add the rice into the pan and start breaking it down with the spatula so that the egg mixes with the rice.
  5. Add the seasonings and fry the rice for about 5 mins.
  6. Plate and enjoy!!
